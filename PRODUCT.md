# PRODUCT.md

## What you're building

A SaaS tool for restaurants and local businesses that turns existing customer reviews into ready-to-post social media content:

- Selects the best review quotes
- Generates captions in the business’ brand voice
- Produces branded graphics (logo/colors/fonts)
- Queues and schedules posts to social channels (via a scheduler integration first, direct publishing later)

## Who it's for

- Restaurants, salons, gyms, local services (high review volume, low marketing time)
- Owners/managers who want consistent posting without hiring an agency
- Multi-location operators who need repeatable content at scale

## Core value proposition

Turn “unused social proof” into a steady stream of on-brand posts with minimal effort:

- Less time writing/creating
- More consistency
- Better trust (real customer words)

## MVP scope

MVP focuses on a tight loop:

1. Ingest reviews (initially manual import; then Google Business Profile/OAuth)
2. Extract & rank candidate quotes
3. Generate 3–5 post drafts per week per business
4. Render branded graphics from templates
5. Approval queue + schedule via a scheduler integration (e.g., Buffer)

## What "done" looks like (MVP success)

- A business can connect/import reviews in under 5 minutes
- The system generates quality drafts with minimal edits needed
- The business can approve and schedule a week of content in <15 minutes
- Posts are consistently branded (logo/colors/fonts)
- Basic audit trail exists (what review became what post, when it was scheduled)

## Non-goals (for MVP)

- Full “supports every social network” direct publishing
- Fully automated posting without approval (we start with human-in-the-loop)
- Advanced analytics / A/B testing (later)
- Multi-user team workflows (later)
