# PRODUCT.md

## What you're building
A lightweight, opinionated framework for capturing product intent, technical direction, and team conventions in a small set of always-current docs.

## Who it's for
- Small engineering teams shipping quickly
- Founders/tech leads who need alignment without heavy process
- Contributors who need clear context before making changes

## What "done" looks like
- Product goals are clear and easy to reference
- Architecture choices are documented and stable
- Team workflow expectations are explicit
- New contributors can understand the project from docs alone
